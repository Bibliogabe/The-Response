The Response: Libraries of Texas

The COVID-19 pandemic has disrupted the personal and professional lives of people around the world, librarians are no exception. However, despite the rapidly evolving information and guidelines, building closures, and loss of resources during the disaster, librarians remained true to their mission as community institutions, continuing to function and respond to the new situation by finding creative ways to serve their communities. Learn how public, academic, and school librarians from Texas responded to the disaster through personal interviews, photographs, and program flyers. This exhibit features some of the ways in which librarians and staff around the country adapt and find new ways to connect and support their patrons and colleagues.
